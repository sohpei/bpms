Notes on format for blogsentences-withbpms.csv:

## blogsentences-withbpms.csv columns

- **sentences** — text of the sentence.
- **Extracted_Pronouns_BPMs** — BPMs preceded by possessive pronouns in the text.
- **has_bpm**, **has_my_bpm**, **has_his_her_their_bpm**, **has_your_bpm** — 0/1 flags for the presence of the pronoun + BPM combination.
- **Extracted_BPMs** — all BPMs in the text.
- **high_valence**, **low_valence**, **high_arousal**, **low_arousal**, **high_dominance**, **low_dominance** — 0/1 flags indicating NRC emotion lexicon matches.
- **high_anger**, **high_fear**, **high_joy**, **high_sadness**, **high_surprise**, **high_trust**, **high_anticipation**, **high_disgust** — 0/1 flags indicating NRC emotion lexicon emotion matches.
